Earnings & Payments

Earnings vary based on the types of tasks you complete, how many tasks are available, the time you dedicate, and your accuracy/quality. Some users earn supplementary income, while others earn more significantly. We emphasize transparency – the payout for each task is clearly stated beforehand.

Once you reach the minimum withdrawal threshold (clearly stated in your dashboard), you can request a payout via supported methods like PayPal or Bank Transfer. Processing times typically range from 3-7 business days, depending on the method selected.

You can update your payment details within your Account Settings or Profile section. Ensure your information is accurate to avoid payment delays. For security reasons, changing payment methods might require additional verification.

Your Inspire IT aims to minimize fees, but some payment processors (like PayPal or banks for international transfers) may impose their own transaction fees. Any potential fees are usually outlined during the withdrawal process. Check the 'Payment Info' section for details specific to each method.

Tasks & Quality

We offer a variety of tasks including, but not limited to:

  • Online surveys
  • Data entry and verification
  • Image tagging and categorization
  • Content moderation
  • Basic transcription
  • Participating in research studies

Task availability depends on client needs and may vary.

Tasks may be rejected if they don't meet the quality standards outlined in the instructions, contain significant errors, or appear to be submitted hastily without proper effort. Please carefully review task guidelines before starting. You can usually find feedback on rejected tasks in your task history or dashboard.

Always read the instructions carefully before starting a task. Pay attention to detail, double-check your work before submitting, and take your time. Avoid rushing. Consistently high-quality work may lead to access to more or better-paying tasks over time.
